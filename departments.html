VOLUNTEERING

A volunteer in FOLC is any child of God who, on the recommendation of the Pastor has been set aside for ​special services in the house of God. Primarily and ultimately, the volunteer is working for God Almighty Himself and it follows then that his/her reward is from the Almighty too.
A volunteer must be born again – John 3:3,5, prayerful & committed to the vision.
